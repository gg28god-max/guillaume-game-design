About

Concept Artist & UI/UX Designer based in Montréal, Canada.

A creative professional with a career built at the intersection of game design, visual storytelling, and technical craft. Currently serving as Lead Concept Artist and UI/UX Designer at Little Guy Games.

From blue-sky ideation to high-fidelity illustration and 3D technical handoff, my practice spans the full production pipeline — whether designing production-ready UI systems in Unreal Engine, building immersive diegetic VR interfaces, or developing world-building assets from the ground up.

Trained at OCAD University with a Bachelor of Design in Illustration, I complement traditional art fundamentals with modern PBR workflows, Substance Painter, ZBrush, Blender, and Unreal Engine 5 implementation. Bilingual in English and French.
